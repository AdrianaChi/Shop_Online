My story

All our dreams can come true, if we have the courage to pursue them.



It all began in the last year of high school with the uniform that I was so tired of.I had to wear dark grey pants made out of cloth... I had a pair of pants that did not have the perfect fit for me so I begin to modify them so they would fit me better.
From there I started to learn more about sewing from my mom who is a dressmaker.

I looked for more information about other styles and what clothes suit different body types. I learned the colors chromatic and I what shades were more suitable for me.Then,that lead me thinking that this can be more than just a passion and somehow I can earn money in the process...
